American Rag

American Rag builds vintage style from the bottom up. Cargo shorts and denim are destructed and embellished with subtle detail for an authentic lived-in look.

Knits

It is a comfort, versatility, and unparalleled style. Polos, crews, ringer tees, and v-necks.

Details

Some piping here, some stitching there, and distressed printed almost everywhere. American Rag pulls out all the stops with a novelty look that is original. Pieces are a time-honored statement of style.

Accessories

Style is all about mixing it up and having fun. Great belts, hats, bags, and flip flops give him all the tools he'll need to assert his personal style.
